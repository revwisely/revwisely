The roles we typically hire

We hire experienced practitioners across three core profiles:

Senior Strategy & Process Consultants

You've lived inside revenue organizations or complex operating environments. You can diagnose messy problems, design pragmatic solutions, and guide teams through change. You move easily between strategy and execution.

Business & Data Analysts

You are fluent in data, systems, and operational detail. You turn ambiguity into structure, insights into decisions, and models into action. You're as comfortable with stakeholders as you are with datasets.

Full-Stack GTM Engineers

You build. You integrate. You make systems work. You're excited by workflow automation, data pipelines, AI-enabled tools, and pragmatic engineering that supports real operators—not demos.
